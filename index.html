Towards Efficient AIGC: The EPIC Lab @ SJTU
Brief Introduction
At the EPIC (Efficient and Precision Intelligent Computing) Laboratory of SJTU, we focus on developing lightweight and efficient models for text-to-image and text-to-video generation. Our goal is to break through the limitations of high computational cost and inference latency, making high-resolution and long-form visual generation both practical and scalable. This enables the broader industrial adoption of AIGC (Artificial Intelligence Generated Content) technologies. This page serves as a project hub for our ongoing work in Efficient AIGC Models.
